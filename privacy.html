Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

At ADULTTOYSKE, your privacy is our most absolute priority. We understand the sensitive nature of our products and have designed our entire operation to protect your confidentiality.

1. Data Collection

We only collect information necessary to process your order, including your name, delivery address, and contact details. We do not sell or share your personal data with any third parties for marketing purposes.

2. Discreet Billing

Your bank or mobile money statement will never show "ADULTTOYSKE." All transactions will appear under a generic merchant name to ensure your billing remains private.

3. Communication

We only contact you regarding your active orders or if you have explicitly subscribed to our newsletter. We will never send promotional material in a way that compromises your privacy.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and secure database management (Supabase) to ensure your profile and order history are protected from unauthorized access.
